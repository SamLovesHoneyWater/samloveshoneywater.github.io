About Sam

I'm an early-career technologist with a passion for building systems that bridge the gap between human cognition and artificial intelligence. My work spans software engineering, machine learning, and AI development, but my curiosity extends far beyond code.

What fascinates me most is the intersection of technology with philosophy, cognitive science, and social systems. Whether I'm designing neural networks inspired by biological processes, analyzing the strategic depth of board games, or thinking about how urban planning shapes human behavior, I'm always looking for connections between seemingly disparate fields.

This site is my space to share projects, ideas, and explorations that I hope will be interesting to both technical professionals and curious minds from all backgrounds.
